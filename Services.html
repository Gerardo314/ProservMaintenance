Services We Offer

We Make Your Property Immaculate—For You and Those Who Matter Most

Why Choose Proserv Maintenance?

We specialize in delivering top-tier commercial cleaning services tailored to meet the unique needs of apartment communities and office spaces. Our comprehensive range of services is designed to ensure that every corner of your property is meticulously cleaned and maintained, from the high-traffic common areas to the intimate spaces within each unit.

Whether you’re moving in, moving out, or simply looking to maintain a spotless environment, our dedicated team is here to provide the professional cleaning solutions you need to keep your property looking its best. Discover how our expertise can enhance the cleanliness and appeal of your space by exploring the services we offer below.

Move-In Cleaning

Moving into a new apartment should be an exciting experience, not a stressful one. Our Move-In Cleaning service ensures that your new space is spotless and ready for you to settle in. We thoroughly clean all rooms, including kitchens, bathrooms, and living areas, ensuring every corner is dust-free, sanitized, and welcoming. Our service includes deep cleaning of appliances, cabinets, floors, windows, and more, so you can move in with peace of mind, knowing your new home is pristine.

Move-Out Cleaning

Leaving an apartment and want to ensure you get your security deposit back? Our Move-Out Cleaning service takes care of everything from top to bottom. We focus on removing any traces of previous occupancy, including deep cleaning carpets, scrubbing bathrooms, wiping down walls, and making kitchens shine. Whether you're a tenant looking to secure your deposit or a landlord preparing for new occupants, our thorough cleaning ensures the space is ready for its next chapter.

Office Cleaning

A clean office environment is crucial for productivity and employee well-being. Our Office Cleaning service caters to the specific needs of commercial spaces, providing daily, weekly, or customized cleaning schedules to keep your workplace tidy and professional. From dusting and vacuuming to sanitizing workstations and common areas, we ensure that your office remains a welcoming space for both employees and clients.

Clubhouse & Amenities Cleaning

The clubhouse and amenities in your apartment complex are key selling points for potential residents. Our Clubhouse & Amenities Cleaning service ensures these shared spaces are always in top condition. We meticulously clean everything from fitness centers and lounges to pools and restrooms, maintaining a high standard of cleanliness that reflects the quality of your property.

Model Apartment Cleaning

First impressions are everything, especially when it comes to showing potential tenants your model apartments. Our Model Apartment Cleaning service guarantees that your show units are always looking their best. We pay attention to every detail, from dusting and polishing surfaces to ensuring bathrooms and kitchens sparkle. This service ensures that your model apartments are always ready to impress, helping you close deals faster.
